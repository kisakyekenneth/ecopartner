Integrated Management System (IMS) with an intension of achieving ISO certification.

Eco & Partner Consult Ltd is currently developing an Integrated Management System (IMS) with an intension of achieving ISO certification. The IMS certification involves the implementation of three ISO standards: ISO 9001 for quality management, ISO 14001 for environmental management, and ISO 45001 for occupational health and safety management. The certification process is intended to evaluate our organization’s ability to effectively manage our operations while meeting these standards.

As an environmental, social, and sustainability firm, we have robust systems and processes in place to ensure we meet our clients’ needs while adhering to strict environmental, social, and safety standards. However, achieving IMS certification reiterates our commitment to continuous improvement and stakeholder engagement.

The ISO 9001 standard focuses on quality management. Eco & Partner has developed, implemented and achieved certification of its Quality Management System. This management system has been in place since October 2017, with the intention of meeting the needs of our clients and stakeholders. Through the implementation of the ISO 9001 standard, we are ensuring that our services meet the highest standards of quality and we are continuously improving to better serve our clients.

The ISO 14001 and 4500 standards are focused on environmental management and health & safety respectively. Eco and Partner has had a Health Safety and Environment management system in place since its founding in 2003, comprising of our policy commitment, plans to achieve our policy objects and means of evaluation of our performance against the set objectives.

We are currently undertaking a process to develop a single system (Integrated Management System) designed to manage these multiple aspects of our operations in line with these multiple standards. In order to avoid duplicating processes or creating unnecessary extra work for our staff.

Ultimately, safety, environmental management and quality control have many common points, and all work towards the goal of making our organization more effective and efficient.

In summary, we aim to meet the highest standards of management and contribute to the sustainability of the planet. IMS certification is a significant achievement that reinforces our commitment to our clients, stakeholders, and the environment.
