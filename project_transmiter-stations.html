Environmental Social Consultancy Services for Installation of Base Transceiver Stations

Eco & Partner Consult Limited provided Environmental and Social Consultancy services for the installation of base transceiver stations (BTS) at various locations around the country for telecommunications companies like Airtel and MTN.

  • Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA) for the proposed BTS locations to ensure compliance with environmental regulations and standards.
  • Social Impact Assessments (SIA) to identify and mitigate potential impacts on local communities.
  • Stakeholder engagement and consultations to gather feedback and address concerns from affected communities.
  • Development of Environmental and Social Management Plans (ESMP) to guide the implementation of mitigation measures.
  • Monitoring and evaluation of environmental and social performance during and after the installation of BTS.
  • Client: Airtel, MTN
  • Location: Various locations across Uganda
  • Service: Environmental and social consultancy
