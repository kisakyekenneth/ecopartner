Hima Cement Limited is a cement manufacturer in Uganda. It is a subsidiary of the Holcim Group, a building materials manufacturer headquartered in Switzerland, with subsidiaries in over 80 countries.

Cement is an essential building material which is used in almost every construction project worldwide. Hima Cement (U) Ltd provides cement to several large scale and smaller scale consumers throughout East Africa. Key raw materials used to produce cement include calcium carbonate, silica, alumina and iron ore. These are extracted from limestone rock.

Hima Cement (U) Ltd currently produces approximately 1480 tons of clinker per day and operates 2 major quarries, the Hima Quarry and the Dura Quarry.

Eco & Partner has provided Environmental and Social Consultancy Services including: an Environmental Social Impact Assessment (ESIA) for the limestone Quarry and the Environmental Social Impact Assessment for a Clinker Grinding Plant.
